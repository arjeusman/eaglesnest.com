Frequently Asked Questions

What is Coliving?

Coliving is a modern form of living that offers an alternative to the old ways of doing things – it’s the answer to complicated rental processes, expensive move-in costs and loneliness and that can often go along with a move to a big city. Coliving offers fully furnished homes with modern touches in convenient locations where community and shared experiences rule.

Who are my potential roommates?

We are E-interviewing everyone to make sure that the house community is comfortable. There many cool people. among them are millennials, newcomers, students, interns, actors, entrepreneurs, start-ups founders, ex-pats, digital nomads, interesting people looking for an affordable option in a shared living space with a vibrant community in Manassas, Virginia.

Do you have options for a couple?

Yes, we allowed couples to stay in any of private room for their choice. We do charge a small additional fee which covers services expense for the additional person in the home.

What if I'm not from the USA and don't have SSN and credit history?

We do not check your credit history. The main factors to be accepted into the community are honesty and transparency, the purpose of your trip, and a clean criminal background record.

Do you have any alternatives for a security deposit?

Yes, we do have a Deposit Free Living Program to help offset the up-front cost to rent and you don’t need to freeze the big amount while you are living at Eagles' Nest.

I made a reservation. What's next?

We make it super easy to move in. Once you sign your lease, you will receive a confirmation email welcoming you to the Eagles' Nest membership. We will include instructions for getting to the home and the procedure for making contact with the house leader who will welcome you to your new home.
