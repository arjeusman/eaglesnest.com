What is a Home?

Home is many things to many different people. For some it is the road, for some, it is a country, for some it is a structure, for some, it is a feeling. It is definitely more than the bricks and mortar. And after the COVID isolation times, we are rethinking how we define “Home”.

Home is a community. It is knowing your neighbor. It is a feeling of safety and support. It has comfortable furnishings and space to live and work. It is the office, the social circle, the emotional support from people around you.

What is Coliving?

Coliving is a pretty new concept born out of the ideas of the sharing economy. It is a place where like-minded people rent private or shared rooms and share common living spaces in houses and apartment buildings.

What is Eagles' Nest?

Our vision is to simplify the difficult process of renting a room in Manassas. We offer fully furnished modern homes for people pursuing their dreams of living and working in NewYork City. We are private and shared spaces in modern fully-furnished homes with affordable prices and flexible leases. We are all-inclusive living. We are move-in ready – sometimes you can move within hours. We are 24/7 Support Teams. We are the answer to the questions arising from the transitions that are occurring. We are the hub for remote workers and work-from-home students and professionals. We are a new way of living. We are made up of our houses, our people, our team, our dreams, and our Manassas City experience.

Eagles' Nest Coliving is leading the revolution in the new definition of “Home”
